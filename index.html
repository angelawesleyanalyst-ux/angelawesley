FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my book marketing services.

What types of authors do you work with?

I work with both fiction and non-fiction authors across virtually all genres. My clients range from debut authors to multi-published writers, self-published to traditionally published. What matters most is your commitment to your book's success and willingness to invest in professional marketing.

What results can I realistically expect?

While I never make guarantees, my clients typically experience a 150-400% increase in monthly sales within the first 90 days, improved Amazon ranking, category bestseller status, and increased review velocity. Results depend on factors like book quality, cover design, genre competitiveness, and budget allocation.

How long does it take to see results?

You will see movement within the first 30 days. Amazon listing optimization shows improved conversion within days, while sustainable sales patterns typically establish within 60-90 days. Long-term, you will experience organic discovery increases and compounding effects of previous efforts.

Do you work with authors outside the United States?

Yes! I work with authors from around the world. Approximately 40% of my clients are international. All consultations happen via video conference with time zone accommodation, and marketing strategies adapt to your primary market.

What if I have already tried marketing and it did not work?

You are not alone. This is one of the most common situations I encounter. Often, previous marketing failures happened because of scattered efforts without cohesive strategy, wrong channels for your specific genre, poor timing, or weak positioning. I will analyze what you have tried, identify why it did not work, and show you exactly what needs to change.

How involved do I need to be in the marketing process?

This depends on the service package you choose. For Done-For-You services, I handle strategy, implementation, and management while you approve plans and receive regular reports (2-3 hours monthly). For Collaborative services, we develop strategy together and share implementation responsibilities (5-8 hours monthly).

Can you help with pre-publication planning?

Absolutely! In fact, pre-publication is often the BEST time to engage a marketing strategist. I can help with title testing, cover design direction, category and keyword strategy, pre-launch platform building, launch timing optimization, and series planning for maximum commercial impact.
